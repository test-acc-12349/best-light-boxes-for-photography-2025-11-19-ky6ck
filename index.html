About Light Box Photography

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by a collective of professional photographers and lighting engineers, Light Box Photography emerged from a simple mission: to democratize access to professional-grade lighting equipment. Our founders recognized that talented photographers were limited not by skill, but by equipment availability and cost. Over the past eight years, we've refined our designs through thousands of hours of testing and feedback from photographers worldwide. Today, we're proud to offer lighting solutions that rival studio equipment costing three times as much, making professional photography accessible to everyone from hobbyists to established studios.

Our Mission

We believe that exceptional photography should not be limited by budget or location. Our mission is to empower photographers at every level by providing innovative, affordable, and reliable lighting solutions that inspire creativity and deliver professional results. We're committed to continuous innovation, sustainability, and building a community of photographers who push the boundaries of what's possible. Every product we create is guided by our core values: quality without compromise, accessibility for all, and unwavering support for our customers' creative journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our light boxes and photography solutions

Our light boxes feature a 5500K daylight color temperature, which is the standard for professional photography and matches natural daylight. This ensures that your photos will look accurate whether you're shooting products, portraits, or any other subject. The flicker-free LED technology also provides consistent, stable lighting throughout your entire shoot, eliminating the color shifts that can occur with traditional lighting equipment. All our lights are calibrated to CRI 95+ for true color reproduction.

Our portable light boxes are designed for quick and easy assembly. Most users can have their complete setup ready in 3-5 minutes. The frame uses a tool-free assembly system with intuitive snap-fit connectors, and the diffusion panels attach securely without any complicated steps. The LED lights are pre-installed and simply plug into standard power outlets. We include detailed setup instructions with every unit, and our customer support team is always available if you need additional guidance. After a few uses, the setup becomes second nature!

The professional quality of our lighting means you'll get excellent results whether you're shooting with a $300 smartphone or a $5,000 professional camera.
